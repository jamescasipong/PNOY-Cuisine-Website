About
The Sisig Spot

At The Sisig Spot, we believe in celebrating the rich and flavorful tradition of Filipino cuisine, with a special focus on one of its most beloved dishes: sisig. Located in the heart of Quezon City, Cubao. Our restaurant is a haven for food enthusiasts seeking an authentic and unforgettable dining experience.


Our journey began with a simple passion for sharing the mouthwatering flavors of sisig with the world. From the first bite, you'll discover why this dish has captured the hearts and taste buds of so many. Each serving is a harmonious blend of succulent pork, savory spices, and a hint of citrus, cooked to perfection on a sizzling platter.

Sisig with Egg

Sisig with egg is a popular Filipino dish featuring finely chopped pork marinated in a tangy blend of calamansi juice, vinegar, and soy sauce. Sautéed with onions, garlic, and chili peppers, the dish is served sizzling hot with a raw egg cracked on top, which cooks slightly from the heat, adding a creamy richness to the spicy, savory flavors.
